Welcome to Sir M.Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology

Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology is one of the top-most colleges in Karnataka with a brilliant faculty and placement record.

Students in this college can enroll into 9 different branches. They are :-

  1. CSE
  2. ISE
  3. ECE
  4. TE
  5. ME
  6. IEM
  7. Civil
  8. EEE
  9. Biotech

Sir MVIT is a located on the outskirts of Bangalore, 14kms from Kempeggowda International Airport

New Block

The main building of Sir MVIT which comprises of the Administrative Office, and the four royal branch - CSE, ISE, EC and TE and also Placement Cell. It is the most live building in the campus with a lot of sweet memories for all the alumni's and students of this prestigious institute.

CSE + ISE

Under the leadership of Prof Dilip K Sen i.e. our esteemed HOD, this branch has witnessed the most amazing placement year after year. This is the branch with the best student-teacher relationship and interaction. With the staff constantly encouraging students to present technical papers or indulge in various sorts of projects, the students get a lot of exposure to "real-world" scenarios.
